Job Summary 

Responsible for regulatory aspects related to product manufacturing, quality, and stability, 
ensuring compliance with global guidelines from agencies like FDA, EMA, and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare and review CMC sections of CTD/eCTD (Module 3)  
• Ensure compliance with GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices)  
• Evaluate manufacturing processes, validation, and stability data  
• Manage post-approval CMC changes (variations, supplements)  
• Support technology transfer and scale-up activities  
• Coordinate with manufacturing, QA, and R&D teams  

Qualifications 

• M.Pharm / MSc (Pharmaceutical Chemistry / Industrial Pharmacy)  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Strong knowledge of GMP & ICH guidelines  
• Manufacturing process understanding  
• Analytical thinking

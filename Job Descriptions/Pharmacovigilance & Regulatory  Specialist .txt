Job Summary 

Integrates drug safety (pharmacovigilance) with regulatory compliance to ensure safe product 
use. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Monitor and report adverse drug reactions (ADRs)  
• Prepare safety reports (PSUR, DSUR)  
• Ensure compliance with pharmacovigilance regulations  
• Coordinate with regulatory teams for safety submissions  
• Support risk management plans (RMP)  
• Participate in signal detection and risk assessment  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / MSc (Pharmacology / Life Sciences)  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Drug safety knowledge  
• Regulatory reporting  
• Data analysis and interpretation

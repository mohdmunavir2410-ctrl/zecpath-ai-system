Job Summary 

Ensures product registration and compliance within India as per CDSCO guidelines. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare and submit regulatory applications (Form 44, CT-XX forms)  
• Handle DCGI approvals and licensing requirements  
• Ensure compliance with Indian Drugs & Cosmetics Act  
• Coordinate with state FDA authorities  
• Manage product registrations and renewals  
• Support inspections and audits  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / MSc  
• 3–8 years experience in Indian regulatory affairs  

Skills 

• Knowledge of Indian regulatory framework  
• Documentation and submission expertise  
• Regulatory liaison skills

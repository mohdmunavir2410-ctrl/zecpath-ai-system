Job Summary 

Manages regulatory registration and approval of medical devices across global markets, 
ensuring compliance with authorities like the FDA, EMA, and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare and submit device registration dossiers (e.g., 510(k), PMA, CE marking)  
• Classify medical devices based on risk (Class I, II, III)  
• Compile technical documentation (Technical File / Design Dossier)  
• Ensure compliance with global regulations (EU MDR, US FDA 21 CFR Part 820)  
• Coordinate with Notified Bodies and regulatory authorities  
• Manage lifecycle activities (renewals, variations, post-market updates)  
• Support audits and inspections  

Qualifications 

• B.E./B.Tech (Biomedical / Mechanical / Biotechnology) or B.Pharm / MSc  
• 3–8 years experience in medical device regulatory affairs  

Skills 

• Knowledge of device regulations (MDR, FDA)  
• Technical documentation & risk classification  
• Communication with regulatory authorities

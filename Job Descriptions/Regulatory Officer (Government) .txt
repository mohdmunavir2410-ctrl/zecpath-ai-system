Job Summary 

Manages regulatory processes, approvals, and compliance monitoring within government 
agencies such as CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Review and approve regulatory submissions (drugs, devices, cosmetics)  
• Evaluate clinical trial applications and product registrations  
• Ensure compliance with national regulations  
• Draft regulatory guidelines and policies  
• Coordinate with industry stakeholders  
• Monitor post-market surveillance activities  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / MSc / PhD (Life Sciences)  
• Government recruitment process  

Skills 

• Regulatory evaluation  
• Policy understanding  
• Decision-making

Job Summary 

Utilizes real-world data (RWD) to support regulatory decisions, submissions, and post
market surveillance. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Analyze real-world data (EHR, claims, registries)  
• Generate real-world evidence for regulatory submissions  
• Support post-marketing surveillance and safety studies  
• Collaborate with clinical and epidemiology teams  
• Prepare RWE reports for regulatory agencies  
• Ensure compliance with data and regulatory standards  

Qualifications 

• MSc / PhD in Epidemiology / Biostatistics / Life Sciences  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Data analytics & statistics  
• Epidemiology knowledge  
• Regulatory understanding

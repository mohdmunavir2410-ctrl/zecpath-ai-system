Job Summary 

Specializes in electronic publishing and submission of regulatory dossiers in eCTD format. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Compile and publish eCTD submissions (Modules 1–5)  
• Validate submission packages using publishing tools  
• Manage submission lifecycle (IND, NDA, ANDA, MAA)  
• Troubleshoot technical issues in submissions  
• Ensure compliance with regional submission standards  
• Maintain submission archives and version control  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Pharmacy / Life Sciences / IT  
• 3–8 years experience in regulatory publishing  

Skills 

• eCTD tools (e.g., Veeva, Lorenz)  
• XML and document formatting  
• Technical problem-solving

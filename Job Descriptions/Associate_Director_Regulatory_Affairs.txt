Job Summary 

Leads regulatory strategy for multiple products or therapeutic areas and supports senior 
leadership in global regulatory planning, ensuring compliance with agencies like FDA, EMA, 
and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Develop regulatory strategies for product portfolios  
• Oversee global submissions (IND, NDA, ANDA, MAA)  
• Manage and mentor regulatory teams  
• Lead interactions with regulatory authorities  
• Ensure compliance with global regulatory frameworks  
• Support due diligence, licensing, and partnerships  

Qualifications 

• M.Pharm / MSc / PhD  
• 12–18 years of experience  

Skills 

• Strategic leadership  
• Regulatory expertise  
• Stakeholder management

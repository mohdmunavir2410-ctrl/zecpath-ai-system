Job Summary 

Provides freelance services for compiling, publishing, and submitting regulatory dossiers in 
eCTD format. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Compile and publish eCTD submissions (Modules 1–5)  
• Validate submission packages using publishing tools  
• Manage lifecycle submissions (initial, variations, renewals)  
• Troubleshoot technical issues in submissions  
• Ensure compliance with regional electronic submission standards  
• Maintain submission archives and version control  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Pharmacy / Life Sciences / IT  
• 3–10 years experience in regulatory publishing  

Skills 

• eCTD tools (Veeva Vault, Lorenz, etc.)  
• XML & document formatting  
• Technical troubleshooting

Job Summary 

Manages regulatory submissions and compliance activities for the United States market in 
accordance with FDA regulations. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare and submit IND, NDA, ANDA, and BLA applications  
• Ensure compliance with US FDA regulations (21 CFR)  
• Respond to FDA queries, deficiency letters, and inspections  
• Manage labeling requirements (USPI, SPL format)  
• Track submission timelines and approvals  
• Collaborate with clinical, QA, and manufacturing teams  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / MSc (Life Sciences)  
• 3–8 years experience in US regulatory affairs  

Skills 

• Strong knowledge of FDA guidelines  
• eCTD submissions  
• Regulatory communication

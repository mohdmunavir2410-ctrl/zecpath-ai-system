Job Summary 

Provides operational support for regulatory compliance and assists in communication with 
regulatory authorities. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Support regulatory submissions (IND, NDA, ANDA basics)  
• Assist in responding to regulatory queries  
• Maintain product registration records  
• Help in labeling compliance and documentation  
• Participate in audits and inspections  

Qualifications 

• Bachelor’s/Master’s in Pharmacy or Life Sciences  
• 0–3 years experience  

Skills 

• Understanding of regulatory pathways  
• Communication & coordination skills  
• Analytical thinking

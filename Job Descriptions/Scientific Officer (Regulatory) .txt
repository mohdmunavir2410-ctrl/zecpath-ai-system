Job Summary 

Provides scientific evaluation of regulatory submissions and supports decision-making within 
regulatory authorities. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Review scientific data (preclinical, clinical, CMC) in submissions  
• Evaluate safety, efficacy, and quality of products  
• Support approval decisions for drugs and biologics  
• Prepare scientific assessment reports  
• Participate in expert committees and advisory panels  
• Monitor emerging scientific and regulatory trends  

Qualifications 

• MSc / PhD (Pharmacology / Biotechnology / Life Sciences)  
• Research or regulatory experience  

Skills 

• Scientific analysis  
• Regulatory knowledge  
• Critical thinking

Job Summary 

Ensures that company processes comply with regulatory and quality standards. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Monitor compliance with SOPs and regulatory requirements  
• Assist in internal audits and inspections  
• Review batch records and quality documentation  
• Identify compliance risks and suggest improvements  
• Support CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Actions)  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / MSc / Biotechnology  
• 0–3 years experience  

Skills 

• Knowledge of GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices)  
• Quality systems understanding  
• Analytical and problem-solving skills

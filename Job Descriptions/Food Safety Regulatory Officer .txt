Job Summary 

Ensures that food products comply with national and international food safety regulations, 
including standards set by FSSAI and global agencies like the FDA. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Ensure compliance with food safety laws and regulations  
• Manage product approvals, licensing, and registrations  
• Review ingredient safety and permissible limits  
• Conduct food labeling compliance checks  
• Coordinate with production, QA, and R&D teams  
• Prepare documentation for audits and inspections  
• Monitor regulatory updates and implement changes  
• Handle food safety audits and inspections  

Qualifications 

• BSc / MSc (Food Technology / Food Science / Nutrition / Life Sciences)  
• 2–6 years experience in food regulatory or quality roles  

Skills 

• Knowledge of food safety regulations (FSSAI, Codex Alimentarius)  
• HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points)  
• Documentation and compliance management  
• Risk assessment and quality control

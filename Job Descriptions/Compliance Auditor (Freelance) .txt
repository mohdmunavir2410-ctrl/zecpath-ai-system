Job Summary 

Conducts independent audits to ensure organizations comply with regulatory and quality 
standards such as GMP, GLP, and GCP. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Perform internal and external audits for clients  
• Evaluate compliance with regulatory guidelines  
• Identify gaps and recommend CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Actions)  
• Prepare detailed audit reports  
• Support regulatory inspections  
• Provide compliance training to organizations  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / MSc / Biotechnology  
• 5–15+ years experience in QA/Regulatory  

Skills 

• Audit expertise  
• Risk assessment  
• Regulatory compliance knowledge

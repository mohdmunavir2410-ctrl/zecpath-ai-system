Job Summary 

Provides scientific and regulatory expertise to support drug development, approval, and 
lifecycle management in compliance with global agencies like FDA, EMA, and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Develop regulatory strategies for drug development programs  
• Prepare and review regulatory documents (IND, NDA, MAA)  
• Interpret scientific data (preclinical, clinical, CMC) for submissions  
• Coordinate with R&D, clinical, and quality teams  
• Ensure compliance with ICH and global regulatory guidelines  
• Support interactions with regulatory authorities  

Qualifications 

• M.Pharm / MSc / PhD (Pharmaceutical Sciences / Life Sciences)  
• 3–10 years experience  

Skills 

• Strong scientific knowledge (pharmacology, clinical research)  
• Regulatory strategy and submission expertise  
• Data analysis and interpretation

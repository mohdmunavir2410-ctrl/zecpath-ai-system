Job Summary 

Handles regulatory requirements for biologics such as monoclonal antibodies, gene therapies, 
and biosimilars. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare BLA (Biologics License Application) submissions  
• Ensure compliance with biologics-specific regulations  
• Review CMC data related to biologics manufacturing  
• Coordinate with R&D and clinical teams  
• Manage biosimilar approvals and regulatory pathways  

Qualifications 

• MSc / PhD in Biotechnology / Life Sciences  
• 3–8 years biologics regulatory experience  

Skills 

• Knowledge of biologics and biosimilars  
• Advanced CMC understanding  
• Regulatory strategy

Job Summary 

Ensures that product labeling complies with regional and global regulatory requirements. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Develop and review labeling content (SmPC, PIL, USPI)  
• Ensure compliance with regulatory labeling guidelines  
• Manage labeling updates and lifecycle changes  
• Coordinate artwork, packaging, and labeling teams  
• Ensure consistency across global markets  
• Maintain labeling databases  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Pharmacy / Life Sciences  
• 3–7 years experience  

Skills 

• Knowledge of labeling regulations  
• Attention to detail  
• Cross-functional coordination

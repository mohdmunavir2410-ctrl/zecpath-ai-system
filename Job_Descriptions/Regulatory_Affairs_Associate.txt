Job Summary 

Supports regulatory submissions and ensures products comply with guidelines from agencies 
like CDSCO, FDA, and EMA. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Assist in preparing regulatory submission documents (CTD/eCTD)  
• Compile and review dossiers for accuracy and completeness  
• Track submission timelines and regulatory approvals  
• Maintain regulatory databases and documentation systems  
• Coordinate with R&D, QA, and clinical teams  
• Monitor regulatory updates and guidelines  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / BSc / MSc (Life Sciences / Biotechnology)  
• 0–2 years experience or freshers  

Skills 

• Documentation & attention to detail  
• Basic regulatory knowledge  
• MS Office, document management systems

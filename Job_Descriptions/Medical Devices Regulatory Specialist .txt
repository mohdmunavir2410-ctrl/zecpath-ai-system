Job Summary 

Ensures regulatory compliance for medical devices across global markets, including device 
classification and approvals. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare submissions (510(k), PMA, CE marking)  
• Ensure compliance with device regulations (e.g., MDR in EU)  
• Manage device classification and risk assessment  
• Coordinate clinical evaluation reports (CER)  
• Support audits and inspections  

Qualifications 

• Biomedical / Life Sciences / Engineering degree  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Knowledge of device regulations  
• Risk management (ISO 14971)  
• Technical documentation

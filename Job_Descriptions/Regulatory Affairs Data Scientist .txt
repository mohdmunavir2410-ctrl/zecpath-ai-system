Job Summary

and decision-making across global markets, aligning with agencies like FDA, EMA, and 
CDSCO.

• Analyze regulatory data (submissions, approvals, timelines)  
• Build predictive models for approval success and timelines  
• Develop dashboards for regulatory performance metrics  
• Integrate real-world and clinical data into regulatory insights  
• Collaborate with regulatory, clinical, and data teams  
• Support AI-driven regulatory decision-making  

Qualifications 

• MSc / PhD in Data Science / Bioinformatics / Life Sciences  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Python/R, SQL  
• Machine learning & statistics  
• Regulatory knowledge + data analytics

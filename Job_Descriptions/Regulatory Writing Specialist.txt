Job Summary 

Specializes in creating high-quality regulatory documents required for product approvals and 
compliance. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Write and review regulatory documents (CTD modules, clinical summaries)  
• Prepare documents like IND, NDA, CSR (Clinical Study Reports)  
• Ensure consistency, accuracy, and compliance with guidelines  
• Collaborate with clinical, regulatory, and scientific teams  
• Maintain document templates and standards  
• Edit and proofread scientific content  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Pharmacy / Life Sciences  
• 3–10 years experience in regulatory or medical writing  

Skills 

• Scientific & technical writing  
• Attention to detail  
• Knowledge of ICH guidelines

Job Summary

Manages regulatory submissions and compliance for vaccines, ensuring safety, efficacy, and 
quality standards. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare regulatory submissions for vaccines (clinical & post-approval)  
• Ensure compliance with global vaccine regulations  
• Review clinical trial and immunogenicity data  
• Coordinate with public health authorities  
• Manage emergency use authorizations (EUA)  

Qualifications 

• MSc / PhD in Microbiology / Immunology / Biotechnology  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Vaccine development knowledge  
• Clinical and regulatory expertise  
• Data interpretation

Job Summary 

Responsible for defining and executing the overall regulatory strategy for the organization or 
business unit. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Lead global regulatory strategy and execution  
• Oversee product approvals and lifecycle management  
• Represent the organization before regulatory authorities  
• Drive regulatory policy and compliance initiatives  
• Manage large regulatory teams and budgets  
• Collaborate with R&D, Clinical, QA, and Commercial teams  

Qualifications 

• Advanced degree (PhD preferred)  
• 15–20 years experience  

Skills 

• Executive leadership  
• Decision-making  
• Regulatory policy expertise

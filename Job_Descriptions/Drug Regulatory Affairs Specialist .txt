Job Summary 

Manages regulatory submissions and compliance for pharmaceutical drugs across global 
markets, ensuring adherence to guidelines from agencies like FDA, EMA, and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare and submit IND, NDA, ANDA dossiers  
• Compile CTD/eCTD submissions (Modules 1–5)  
• Manage product lifecycle (variations, renewals, supplements)  
• Respond to regulatory queries and deficiency letters  
• Ensure compliance with ICH guidelines  
• Coordinate with clinical, QA, and manufacturing teams  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / MSc (Pharmaceutical Sciences)  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Drug development knowledge  
• Regulatory submission expertise  
• Scientific writing

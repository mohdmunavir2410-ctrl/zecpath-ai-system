Job Summary 

Leads and manages regulatory start-up activities for clinical trials, ensuring timely site 
activation and regulatory approvals. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare and submit Clinical Trial Applications (CTA/IND)  
• Manage start-up activities (site selection, ethics approvals, regulatory submissions)  
• Coordinate with global regulatory teams and local authorities  
• Track timelines for site activation and approvals  
• Handle country-specific regulatory requirements  
• Support contract and budget negotiations during start-up  
• Ensure compliance with GCP and regulatory standards  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / MSc (Clinical Research / Life Sciences)  
• 2–6 years experience in clinical trial start-up  

Skills 

• Clinical trial start-up expertise  
• Project and timeline management  
• Regulatory submission knowledge  
• Cross-functional coordination

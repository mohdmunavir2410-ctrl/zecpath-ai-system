Job Summary 

Ensures that drugs manufactured, distributed, and sold comply with legal and safety standards 
under authorities like CDSCO and state drug control departments. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Inspect pharmaceutical manufacturing units, pharmacies, and warehouses  
• Enforce the Drugs & Cosmetics Act and related regulations  
• Collect drug samples for testing and quality analysis  
• Investigate complaints related to substandard or counterfeit drugs  
• Initiate legal action against violations  
• Ensure proper storage, labeling, and distribution practices  
• Conduct raids and surveillance operations  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / PharmD  
• Government exam qualification (e.g., UPSC/State PSC)  

Skills 

• Strong regulatory and legal knowledge  
• Inspection and auditing skills  
• Analytical and investigative ability

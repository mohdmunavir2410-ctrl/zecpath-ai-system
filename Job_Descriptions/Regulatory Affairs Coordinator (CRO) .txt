Job Summary 

Provides operational and administrative support for regulatory activities in clinical trials, 
ensuring compliance with global regulations and timely submissions to authorities like FDA, 
EMA, and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Assist in preparation and submission of regulatory documents (CTA, IND, ethics 
submissions)  
• Maintain regulatory binders (Trial Master File – TMF/eTMF)  
• Track submission timelines and approvals across sites  
• Coordinate with sponsors, investigators, and ethics committees  
• Maintain regulatory databases and documentation systems  
• Support audit and inspection readiness  
• Ensure compliance with GCP (Good Clinical Practice) guidelines  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / BSc / MSc (Life Sciences / Clinical Research)  
• 1–4 years experience in CRO or clinical research  

Skills 

• Strong organizational and coordination skills  
• Knowledge of clinical trial regulations  
• Documentation and compliance expertise  
• Communication with multiple stakeholders

Job Summary 

Handles regulatory compliance for dietary supplements, functional foods, and nutraceutical 
products. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Ensure compliance with food and supplement regulations (e.g., FSSAI in India)  
• Manage product approvals and registrations  
• Review ingredient safety and health claims  
• Ensure labeling compliance  
• Coordinate with quality and production teams  

Qualifications 

• BSc / MSc (Food Science / Nutrition / Life Sciences)  
• 3–8 years experience  

Skills 

• Knowledge of nutraceutical regulations  
• Food safety standards  
• Documentation and compliance

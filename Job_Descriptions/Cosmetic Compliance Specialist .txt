Job Summary

Ensures that cosmetic and personal care products comply with regulatory standards for 
safety, labeling, and marketing claims across different regions. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Ensure compliance with cosmetic regulations (e.g., EU Cosmetics Regulation, FDA 
guidelines)  
• Review ingredient safety and restricted substances  
• Manage product registration and notification processes  
• Oversee labeling, packaging, and claims substantiation  
• Coordinate with R&D and marketing teams for compliant product development  
• Maintain Product Information Files (PIF)  
• Support audits and regulatory inspections  
• Track global regulatory changes and update compliance processes  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Pharmacy / Chemistry / Life Sciences  
• 2–6 years experience in cosmetics or personal care industry  

Skills 

• Cosmetic regulatory knowledge  
• Ingredient safety assessment

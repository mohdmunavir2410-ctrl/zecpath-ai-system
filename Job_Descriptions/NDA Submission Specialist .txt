Job Summary 

Specializes in preparing and managing Investigational New Drug (IND) and New Drug 
Application (NDA) submissions for regulatory approval. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Compile and submit IND and NDA applications (CTD/eCTD format)  
• Manage submission lifecycle (initial, amendments, supplements)  
• Ensure completeness and accuracy of submission dossiers  
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams for data collection  
• Respond to regulatory queries and deficiency letters  
• Track submission status and approval timelines  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Pharmacy / Life Sciences  
• 3–8 years experience in regulatory submissions  

Skills 

• IND/NDA submission expertise  
• eCTD publishing knowledge  
• Attention to detail and compliance

Job Summary 

Independently manages regulatory submissions and ensures compliance with global 
regulatory authorities like FDA, EMA, and CDSCO. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Prepare, review, and submit regulatory dossiers (CTD/eCTD)  
• Manage lifecycle submissions (variations, renewals, supplements)  
• Respond to regulatory queries and deficiency letters  
• Coordinate with cross-functional teams (R&D, QA, Clinical)  
• Ensure compliance with ICH guidelines and local regulations  
• Track submission timelines and approvals  

Qualifications 

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / MSc (Life Sciences)  
• 3–6 years of regulatory experience  

Skills 

• Regulatory submission expertise  
• Strong communication with authorities  
• Problem-solving and regulatory strategy

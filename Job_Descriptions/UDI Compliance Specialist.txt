(Unique Device Identification) 
Job Summary 

Ensures compliance with Unique Device Identification (UDI) regulations, enabling 
traceability and safety of medical devices in global markets. 

Key Responsibilities 

• Implement and manage UDI systems for device identification  
• Ensure compliance with UDI regulations (FDA UDI, EU MDR UDI)  
• Assign and maintain UDI codes (DI & PI – Device Identifier & Production Identifier)  
• Submit UDI data to global databases (e.g., GUDID, EUDAMED)  
• Coordinate labeling updates with UDI requirements  
• Monitor changes in UDI regulations and ensure compliance  
• Support audits and regulatory inspections  

Qualifications 

• Degree in Engineering / Life Sciences / Pharmacy  
• 3–7 years experience in regulatory or labeling roles  

Skills 

• UDI systems & database management  
• Knowledge of global UDI regulations  
• Data accuracy & compliance tracking
